Repair & Restoration

Preserving the Value and Elegance of Your Finest Timepieces and Jewelry.

At Tigris Jewelry, we understand that your watches and jewelry are more than just accessories—they are valuable investments and cherished heirlooms. Our expert artisans provide precision maintenance and restoration services right here within the US Embassy compound.

Our Specialized Services Include:

  • Watch Link Adjustment & Sizing: Professional resizing for luxury watches to ensure a flawless, comfortable fit on your wrist.
  • Ring Resizing & Scaling: Precision scaling for gold and silver rings, adjusting sizes up or down without compromising the structural integrity or engraving of the piece.
  • Jewelry Soldering & Laser Welding: Repairing broken chains, necklace clasps, and fractured bracelet links with seamless precision.
  • Stone Tightening & Replacement: Securing loose diamonds, zircons, or heritage stones, and replacing missing crystals to restore original brilliance.
  • Professional Ultra-Sonic Cleaning & Polishing: Removing years of microscopic wear, tarnish, and dust to bring back the mirror-like shine of your precious metals.
